## Antivirus Sources

**LINK:** [Ensure Your antivirus and Anti Malware are Active](https://www.cisa.gov/resources-tools/training/ensure-your-os-antivirus-and-anti-malware-protections-are-active)

**Content:**
These CISA resources explain the importance of keeping operating systems, applications, and security software up to date. They describe how outdated software creates vulnerabilities and emphasize enabling antivirus and anti-malware protections.

**Value:**
CISA is the primary U.S. federal authority on cybersecurity, making these sources authoritative and reliable. The guidance reflects current national cybersecurity standards and best practices.

**Audience:**
Intended for general users, beginners, and home users who want to improve device security with minimal technical expertise.

**Limitations:**
The guidance is high-level and does not include platform-specific configuration steps or advanced technical detail.
