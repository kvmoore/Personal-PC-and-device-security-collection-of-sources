**Content:**  
This article covers router passwords, encryption, and firmware updates. It explains how unsecured wireless networks invite intrusions and unauthorized access. Step-by-step improvements are outlined to help users quickly strengthen protection.

**Value:**  
The information is practical and written in plain language. It empowers consumers to improve network security without needing advanced technical knowledge.

**Audience:**  
Homeowners and renters are the key readers. It is especially useful for beginners setting up or maintaining home internet.

**Limitations:**  
Advanced network segmentation or enterprise features are not discussed. Business users may require deeper technical material.
